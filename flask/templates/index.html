Welcome to UTSC Path Builder

We will help you plan out your academic pathway at the University of Toronto Scarborough Campus!

About This Tool

UTSC Path Builder is designed to simplify your academic planning process. Our interactive tools help you:

  • Track your completed and planned courses
  • Understand complex course prerequisites
  • Verify program requirements for graduation
  • Make informed decisions based on peer ratings and feedback
  • Rate a course that you had taken before
  • Discover new courses that align with your interests and career goals

Getting Started

To begin planning your academic journey:

  • Log in to your account to access personalized features
  • Add your completed courses to "My Courses"
  • Explore course prerequisites for subjects you're interested in
  • Check program requirements to ensure you're on track to graduate

Need help? Feel free to contact the UTSC Academic Advising Centre for additional support.
